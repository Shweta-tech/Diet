{% trans "Nutrition Advocates" %}

{% blocktrans %}We are a team of nutrition academics exploring innovative strategies using ICT to communicate the message of diet-diversity to schools, the community of parents and adults in general. Our aim is to reach out to all the vulnerable populations. Community Nutritionist and Researchers, Aarti Jain and Rima Ved under the guidance of Dr (Mrs.) Ratnaraje Thar, Technical ChiNu at CTARA are striving to reduce the micronutrient dietary gap by promoting diet diversity knowledge and practices; with a view to communicate a behavioral change in the attitudes of the community. {% endblocktrans %}



{% trans "CommunityGIS" %}

{% blocktrans %}CommunityGIS is a Free and Open Source platform for community ownership of data and code for geographic data. Technically communityGIS is a platform for a spatial data infrastructure. This consists of an application (with license GPLv3) frontend as GUI using python Django and Javascripts while the backend is geonode the code is available at github. This platform can be used with modifications for other themes. The preliminary instructions to install and use will be made available soon. This platform of CommunityGIS has been developed by teams from makerghat and CTARA IIT Bombay by Mr Animesh Nautiyal, Ms Himanshi Goyal, Ms Shweta Panwar, Unnati Mishra and team under the guidance of Prof Jitendra Shah, ex-faculty VJTI, now consultant at CTARA IITB.{% endblocktrans %}
